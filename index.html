What is whey protein isolate?

Whey protein isolate is the purest form of whey protein, containing 90%+ protein with minimal lactose, fat, and carbohydrates. It's rapidly absorbed and ideal for post-workout recovery.

Are your products third-party tested?

Yes, every batch of our protein supplements is tested by independent laboratories for purity, potency, and banned substances. We provide certificates of analysis for complete transparency.

What is the best time to take protein supplements?

The best time to take protein supplements is within 30 minutes post-workout to maximize muscle recovery and growth. However, they can also be taken throughout the day to meet your protein intake goals.

Can women take protein supplements?

Absolutely! Protein supplements are beneficial for women as they are for men. They support muscle recovery, weight management, and overall health. Women can benefit from the same high-quality protein sources, just in appropriate serving sizes to meet their individual needs.

Are there any side effects of taking protein supplements?

When taken as directed, protein supplements are safe for most people. However, excessive protein intake can lead to digestive issues, kidney damage, and other health problems. It's important to use protein supplements to complement your diet, not replace whole foods. Always consult with a healthcare provider before starting any new supplement regimen.
